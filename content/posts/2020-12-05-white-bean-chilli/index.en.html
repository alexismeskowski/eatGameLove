Hello friends, here is a vegan style of one of our most favorite chillis to make, white bean chilli!

Original Recipe: https://www.pillsbury.com/recipes/creamy-white-chicken-chili/bb83f142-b7d4-48a0-bc5c-98641a203783

We have changed this recipe quite a bin over the years. We have found that this recipe still tastes AMAZING when made without chicken.



Amount of servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 4.0 cups vegetable broth
  • 2.0 cans of great northern beans
    • We went with a bean/lentil mix we are using up
  • 2.0 medium sized potatoes
  • 1.0 medium onion
  • 3.0 cloves of garlic
  • 4.5 oz of green chillis
    • We bought a huge jar at costco
  • 1.5 tsp cumin
  • 2.0 cups dairy free pepper jack cheese
  • 0.75 cup dairy free sour scream
  • 1.0 handful of spinach
  • 1.0 pinch of salt
  • 1.0 pinch of pepper

Optional

  • corn
  • avocado
  • what ever spices you have on hand!

Preparation

  1. Start a large pot on medium heat and toss in a little olive oil
  2. Chop the garlic and onion, then add to the pot
  3. Scrub and wash the potatoes. Proceed to chop them into 1/2 inch cubes, set aside
  4. Once the onions are translucent add the vegetable broth, beans, potatoes, corn, chiles, and all of the wanted spices. Turn the heat up to medium high to bring to a boil
  5. Once the pot is boiling turn the heat down and simmer for 20 minutes while occasionally sturring
  6. After 20 minutes begin to gradually add in the cheese while stirring
  7. Add in the spinach and sour cream
  8. Cover and let the spinach cook down, about 10 minutes


Notes:

  • Avocado can be added as a topping once you have scooped the chilli into a bowl
